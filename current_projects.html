Current Projects

Widevision VR

Widevision VR is a project to bring Western Sydney University's Widevision 2021 to the greater community during the pandemic and lockdowns. The project was proposed to the 2021 Honors group, and - - chose to take it on.

Wood Keyboard

The wood based mechanical keyboard has been an ongoing project since 2020 when lockdown first hit the Sydney area. The idea has been worked on along side - -'s university work, and is looking to be the 1st product launched under the "Algebuckina Design" name. The plan is to follow the keyboard with a full suite of computer and technological peripherals all with the same design language.

This Website
