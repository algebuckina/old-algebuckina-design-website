Past Projects

The Fold Up Desk

The fold up desk was a project to answer apartment size shrinkage and the increase of property price now and in the future. The desk folds up to a shelving unit, so it takes up no space when not in use, and can still store things on it in both configurations

image

The Blast Bus Shelter

The Blast Bus Shelter was a group project to build a quick construct bus shelter for Anglicare Villages in Castle Hill. The brief requested was to use sheet metal for the some or all of the build. Our group, Bus Boys, chose to create a design that harked back to 60s angular design and a colour scheme that fit the buildings of the area.

image

Reverse Engineered Drill

To solidify my use of solidworks, this project was to deconstruct a drill and recreate it in it's entirety in 3D. A Bosche battery drill was used and it was recreated in Solidworks using surfacing and solid modeling. The final model is an exact 1 to 1 recreation of the drill.
