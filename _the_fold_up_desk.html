The Fold Up Desk

Introduction

The fold up desk was designed to combat the growing housing density crisis seen around the world. (some facts about the housing thingo)

Desk Mode

The desk uses gravity and metal rods to hold itself together when fully colappsed, not needing any clips or restraints, and joining flush with each plank, and these calculations can be seen below.

Shelf Mode

The desk then folds up against the wall into a shelving unit, clipping to the wall, that can store the contents of the desk. Removing the previous issue of fold away desks not having anywhere to store its contents, and increasing the personalisation of a small user space, without limiting practicality.
