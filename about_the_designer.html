About the Designer and Founder

Hey, I'm Bradley Turner, and I am currently the lead designer and CEO at Algebuckina Design. I also work as a delivery driver for Kellyville Flower Spot and volunteer at Saint Matthew's Uniting Church in Baulkham Hills as a youth and young adults leader. I graduated Western Sydney University with an Honours Degree in Industrial Design with a GPA of (insert GPA here).

At Algebuckina Design, I do everything from project design to manufacturing. My normal day at the company consists of checking up on online orders, placed the day before, ordering materials to fill those orders, then laser cutting and soldering up previous orders. When this is done, I ship orders off and then work on any product designs I have in the works or update posts on social media. When I'm not doing work for the company, I am doing one of my side jobs or streaming on twitch.tv.

I've always loved designing and making things, but with a technical spark. This can be seen in my past projects page which can be viewed HERE (link). Of note are my Western Sydney University Widevision project where I created the physical event in a covid safe virtual space as well as my folding desk that made a shelving unit fold down into a desk and was sturdy with no latches or pins.

Now, I am looking to push my design abilities by designing and creating sustainable Australian made peripherals and electronics with locally sourced materials, grow my small company and look for opportunities with ventures in other Industrial Design spaces in Australia.
